Languages

I have experience with a wide variety of languages. Those languages which I am indicating here as being less familiar with have received that rating due to a lack of practical application not time spent learning them. I love functional languages but I generally think it is more difficult to communicate my ideas to other when that medium is used. I still use functional ideas in my code but now prefer the structure and interface clarity that comes with object oriented design.

Frontend Frameworks

I have worked with several more smaller web frameworks other than the ones listed here but these are the most relevant ones still remaining today. Of the three I am most interested in Flutter both for its effortless cross-platform capabilities and rich set of built-in components. The flutter layout system can be a pain sometimes but often it is more intuitive than CSS.

Database

I have desgined several databases for various projects though I'm not an expert. My understanding of SQL is passable but I've much more to learn about that technology.
